## The 10 Components (Named Specifically)

1. **sh-title** - Red title bar at the top with white text
2. **sh-pagebutton** - Top navigation page buttons (white cards with black text)
3. **sh-mainbutton** - Standard red button used throughout pages
4. **sh-register** - Dark red registration button (darker than main button)
5. **sh-sideinformation** - Sidebar information component (white card with red accent)
6. **sh-paragraphcard** - White cards with paragraph content
7. **sh-sponser** - Sponsor display boxes
8. **sh-dropdownpagebuttons** - Dropdown menus that appear on hover over page buttons
9. **sh-onscreenpage** - Sidebar navigation showing current page (bold active page)
10. **sh-cardgrid** - 3x3 grid for displaying cards (photos, games, stats, or text)
